Tomorrowland is a Belgian electronic dance music festival held in Boom, Belgium. It is one of the largest and most notable music festivals in the world, known for its elaborate stage designs and impressive lineups.

History

Tomorrowland was first held in 2005 and has since become one of the most popular music festivals in the world. It is known for its elaborate stage designs and impressive lineups.
